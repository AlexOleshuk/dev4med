Our device counts the shaped elements of blood and calculates the leukocyte formula. This device is equipped with an optical sensor (an optical sensor system) that allows to count the shaped elements of blood with a coefficient of variation from 5 to 9 percent. In addition to the above, the described device is equipped with a pulse oximeter function.

Blood sampling and its analysis are the main components of determining the state of human health taking into account a wide variety of existing diseases in the world.

Forms of pathology accompanied by certain types of absolute leukocytosis

Leukocyte formula shows the information about the level of white blood cells. Basophils, neutrophils, eosinophils change in response to the development of pathology.

The causes of basophilia are the following diseases: for instance, hemophilia, leukemia, autoimmune endocrinopathy, anaphylactic allergic reactions, chronic malignant myeloproliferative diseases.

The causes of neutrophilia: acute purulent inflammatory processes, myocardial infarction, affective states, acute hemolysis of erythrocytes.

The causes of eosinophilia: parasitic diseases, some skin diseases (psoriasis, eczema).

The causes of monocytosis: acute infectious bacterial and viral diseases (tuberculosis, diphtheria).

The causes of lymphocytosis: chronic and acute bacterial infections (whooping cough, brucellosis).
